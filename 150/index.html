An Invitation

An Invitation from Good Hope

For 150 years, by God’s grace, this congregation has gathered to worship Christ, study Scripture, and care for our neighbors in Westlake. Through storms and seasons, generations have come and gone. Yet the Gospel has not changed, and neither has our God.

This June, we invite you to celebrate with us. Whether you have called Good Hope home for decades or you have never set foot in our sanctuary, you are welcome at every gathering. Come join us in giving thanks to God for what He has done, and trusting Him for what He will do next.
