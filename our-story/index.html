Our Story

150 Years of God’s Faithfulness

We acknowledge the church as God’s household, with Jesus Christ as its sure foundation and Chief Cornerstone. With deep gratitude, we honor the individuals and ministries that have served in His name throughout the generations.

Timeline

Timeline of God’s Faithfulness

  1. 1876

    Founded

    The Vision Takes Root

    Good Hope Missionary Baptist Church was established at Mt. Zion Baptist Church in Mossville, driven by a desire to create a place of worship in the Westlake community. Construction began under Reverend Henry Roy. Worship was marked by spirited singing, sound Biblical preaching, and genuine love for one another.

  2. 1918–1919

    Tested by Storm

    Renewed in Spirit

    A storm destroyed the church building in 1918. Though the physical structure was lost, the spirit of the congregation endured. Under Pastor Martin Haynes, the church was faithfully rebuilt in 1919.

  3. 1949

    Strengthening

    Strengthening the Foundation

    The roof was replaced, walls reinforced, rooms added, and a baptismal pool installed. A tangible expression of a growing and committed congregation.

  4. 1959

    Expanding the Land

    Room for the Growing Ministry

    Additional land was purchased and further renovations were completed, making room for the growing ministry God was building.

  5. 1982–2002

    New Leadership

    Pastor Madison Clark, Jr. — A Season of Growth

    Beginning in 1982, Pastor Clark led with sincere love and devotion. Under his leadership the current sanctuary was dedicated (1986), a new parking lot was constructed (1988), the church was incorporated as Good Hope Missionary Baptist Church of Westlake, LA, Inc. (2001), and the educational facility and fellowship hall were built and dedicated (2002).

  6. 2005

    Hurricane Rita

    Tested and Repaired

    Hurricane Rita damaged the roof, siding, and ceiling, temporarily suspending services for three weeks. Repairs were completed and worship joyfully resumed.

  7. Aug 4, 2015

    In Memoriam

    Rev. Madison Clark, Jr. — Eternal Rest

    After thirty-three years of dedicated service, Pastor Clark received his eternal reward. His legacy includes the construction and incorporation of the church, the establishment of the Second Harvest Ministry, and a profound spiritual impact on the congregation he so dearly loved. The fellowship hall was dedicated in his honor as the Rev. Madison Clark, Jr. Fellowship Hall.

  8. Jan 3, 2016

    New Shepherd

    Pastor Martin Timothy “Tim” Ceasar

    On January 3, 2016, Pastor Ceasar delivered his first sermon as the new shepherd of Good Hope. Under his leadership, the church has continued to grow, thrive, and reach new generations.

  9. 2020–2021

    Pandemic & Hurricane Laura

    Faithful Through the Storm

    On March 15, 2020, in-person worship paused as COVID-19 necessitated a shift to virtual ministry via Zoom and Facebook. On August 27, 2020, Hurricane Laura brought catastrophic damage to Westlake. After nearly two years of virtual worship, in-person services joyfully resumed in December 2021.

  10. Jun 22, 2024

    Rededicated

    Sanctuary Rededicated

    Major renovations were completed including upgrades to lighting, audio/visual systems, surveillance, and internet infrastructure. The Rededication Worship Service was held on June 22, 2024, presided over by the New Light Missionary & Educational Baptist Association, with the sermon delivered by President Samuel C. Tolbert of the National Baptist Convention of America International, Inc.

  11. 2026

    150 Years

    Faith · Hope · Legacy

    150 years debt free, mortgage burned. We celebrate God’s faithfulness from June 3 through June 28, 2026.
