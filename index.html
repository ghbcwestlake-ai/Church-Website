Our Heritage

1876

A century and a half of gathering in Westlake.

Good Hope Missionary Baptist Church was established in 1876, born out of Mt. Zion Baptist Church in Mossville and built in the Westlake community under the leadership of Reverend Henry Roy. For 150 years, this congregation has weathered storms, literal and otherwise. The original building was destroyed in a 1918 storm and faithfully rebuilt. Hurricane Rita in 2005 and Hurricane Laura in 2020 each tested the congregation's resolve.

Through every season, Good Hope has remained: same Gospel, same God, new chapter. In 2024, the sanctuary was rededicated after major renovations. In 2026, we celebrate 150 years: debt free, mortgage burned, and looking forward to what God will do next.

What We Believe

We hold to the historic Christian faith as expressed in the Baptist Faith & Message. Here's the short version.

  1. 01

    The Bible

    Scripture is the inspired, trustworthy Word of God, our highest authority for faith and daily life.

  2. 02

    One God in Three Persons

    We worship one God, eternally existing as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

  3. 03

    Jesus Christ

    Fully God and fully man, crucified for our sin, raised on the third day, and returning in glory.

  4. 04

    Salvation by Grace

    Forgiveness and new life come as a gift, received through faith in Christ alone, not earned.

  5. 05

    The Church

    A family of believers gathered for worship, baptism, the Lord's Supper, and serving one another and the world.

Meet Our Pastor

Rev. Martin Timothy “Tim” Ceasar

Senior Pastor · Serving since December 2015

Pastor Tim Ceasar answered the call to Gospel Ministry in 1994 and was ordained in 1998. Before being called to Good Hope in December 2015, he served as Pastor of St. Luke Missionary Baptist Church in Lake Charles for nearly fifteen years.

He holds advanced degrees from McNeese State University and the University of Louisiana Monroe, and serves the community as a Child Welfare Supervisor with Calcasieu Parish. He and his wife Vanessa have three daughters and one granddaughter.
